How it works

The app scores profiles from numeric activity and language features—similar to classic Twitter-style datasets. It does not read your messages or photos.

Input

Counts such as posts, followers, following, favourites, listed, plus a language code. You can paste numbers manually, upload a CSV, or use live fetch where configured.

Models

Pick Random Forest, SVM, or a small neural network. Train on your data/ CSVs when you need fresh weights.

Limits

Scores are estimates from training data—not proof of intent. Use them as one signal alongside platform tools and common sense.

Educational / research use. Do not use outputs as sole grounds for blocking or reporting accounts.
